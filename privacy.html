Privacy Policy — Kids' Learning Apps

Applies to the Kids' Learning Apps suite (Numbers, Letters, and Math).
Publisher: Digital Legends LLC (Texas, USA) · Contact: randy@digital-legends-llc.com
Effective date: [set at publish]

Plain-language summary: These apps collect nothing. They work fully offline, show no ads, use no tracking, and send no data anywhere. There are no accounts and no in-app purchases.

What we collect

None. The Kids' Learning Apps do not collect, store off your device, or transmit any personal information. They do not use analytics, advertising, tracking, accounts, logins, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

On-device data

The apps save your child's learning progress (for example, which numbers or letters have been practiced) only on your device, using the browser/app local storage. This information never leaves the device and is not transmitted to us or anyone else. You can erase it at any time from Grown-ups → Reset progress, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy (COPPA & Google Play Families)

These apps are designed for young children and are built to be COPPA-compliant by design. Because we collect no personal information and transmit nothing, there is nothing to consent to share. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. There is no behavioral advertising, no profiling, and no contact with the child.

Permissions

The apps request no sensitive permissions — no location, no contacts, no camera, no microphone, and no device identifiers. Spoken number and letter audio is played from small bundled sound files and the device's built-in text-to-speech, processed on-device. The apps do not record audio.

Ads, purchases & links

No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions. No in-app purchases. There are no outbound links in a child's path; a simple grown-ups area is protected by a parental gate.

Data sharing & selling

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties — because no user data is ever collected or transmitted.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here and reflected in the app store listing. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
