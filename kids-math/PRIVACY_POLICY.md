# Privacy Policy — DL Math (Add & Subtract)

**Publisher:** Digital Legends LLC (Texas) · **Contact:** randy@digital-legends-llc.com
**App:** DL Math — Add & Subtract

> **Plain-language summary:** This app collects **nothing**. It works fully offline, shows **no ads**, uses **no microphone**, and sends **no data** anywhere.

## What we collect
**None.** DL Math does not collect, store off-device, or transmit any personal information. No analytics, advertising, tracking, accounts, microphone, or third-party SDKs.

## On-device data
The app saves your child's **learning progress** (which math facts they've practiced) and **rewards** (stickers, badges, day-streak count) **only on this device** (local storage). It never leaves the device and is not personal information. Erase anytime: **Grown-ups → Reset progress**, or by uninstalling.

## Children's privacy (COPPA)
Designed for young children and **COPPA-compliant by design**: because we collect no personal information and transmit nothing, there is nothing to consent to share.

## Permissions
**No sensitive permissions** (no location, contacts, camera, **microphone**, or device identifiers). Audio uses the device's built-in text-to-speech, processed on-device.

## Ads & purchases
**No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions.**
